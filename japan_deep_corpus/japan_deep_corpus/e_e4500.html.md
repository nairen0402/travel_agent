The capital of [Kumamoto Prefecture](/list/e1244.html), Kumamoto (熊本) is a major city on [Kyushu](/list/e1108.html)'s west coast with a population of around 750,000. The city is most famous for its [castle](e4501.html), which is one of Japan's largest and most complete. The city was hit by [strong earthquakes in 2016](/news/0035.html).
