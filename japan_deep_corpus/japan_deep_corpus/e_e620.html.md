Japanese cuisine (和食, washoku) offers an abundance of gastronomical delights with a boundless variety of regional and seasonal [dishes](e2035.html). Restaurants in Japan range from mobile food stands to centuries old ryotei, atmospheric drinking places, seasonally erected terraces over rivers and inexpensive chain shops. Many [restaurants](e2036.html) are specialized in a single type of dish, while others offer a variety of dishes.
