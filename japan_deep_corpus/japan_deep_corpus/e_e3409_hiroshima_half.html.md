The following is a suggested half day itinerary that starts in nearby [Miyajima](e3401.html) and takes you on a walking tour around [Hiroshima](e2160.html)'s main attractions. Those not starting in Miyajima are advised to take a tram from Hiroshima Station to the [peace park](e3400.html) and join the itinerary from there instead.

Start

Start at **Miyajima**

**Boat**

45 minutes

Peace Memorial Park

Commemorating the drop of the atomic bomb
30 minutes

The cenotaph is marked by a large arch and is dedicated to those who lost their lives due to the nuclear bomb. The Peace Flame lies beyond and is kept burning until the last of nuclear weapons have been dismantled. Not far from the Cenotaph is the Children's Peace Monument with thousands of origami cranes.

Peace Memorial Museum

Museum about the atomic bombing
60-90 minutes

The sobering [Peace Memorial Museum](e3400.html) exhibit personal details, artefacts and stories of the victims of the nuclear bombing.

**Walk**

5 minutes

Atomic Bomb Dome

Remains of a building that did not collapse
15 minutes

The structure known as the [A-Bomb Dome](e3400.html) used to be the former Prefectural Industrial Promotion Hall. It was one of the few buildings to remain standing after the explosion despite having stood only a hundred meters from the hypocenter. Today it stands as a memorial and reminder of the painful past.

**Walk**

25 minutes

Hiroshima Castle

Reconstructed castle
45 minutes

[Hiroshima Castle](e3402.html) is an example of a [castle](e2296.html) built on a plain as opposed to one built on hills or mountaintops. The original castle was destroyed by the atomic bomb and the current reconstruction was completed 1958. The main keep exhibits [Hiroshima](e2160.html)'s and the castle's history, and visitors can enjoy walking on the castle walls around the keep.

**Walk**

10 minutes

Shukkeien Garden

Japanese landscape garden
45 minutes

[Shukkeien Garden](e3403.html) is a [Japanese landscape garden](e2099.html) that has a history dating back to 1620. The garden offers a number of scenic views and walking trails that wind around the pond in the middle of the garden. Seasonal flora can also be seen at Shukkeien, and there are also tea houses to enjoy a break with a view.

**Walk**

15 minutes

Finish

Hiroshima Station
