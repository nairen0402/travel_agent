Hikone (彦根) is a small city at the shores of Lake Biwa, Japan's largest lake. The city is most famous for its [castle](e7001.html), one of only five castles in Japan designated as national treasures. Hikone makes a pleasant stop-over on a trip between [Tokyo](e2164.html) and [Kyoto](e2158.html) or a side trip from Kyoto.
