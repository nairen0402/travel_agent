The Hakodate Morning Market (函館朝市, Hakodate Asaichi) is held daily from 5am (6am from January to April) to 2pm, just a few steps away from JR Hakodate Station. The [market](e2452.html) area spans about four city blocks.

Products on sale include various types of fresh seafood, such as crabs (kani), salmon eggs (ikura) and sea urchin (uni), as well as fresh produce such as melons. Many [restaurants](e2036.html) can be found in the market area, offering fresh seafood breakfasts, such as uni-ikura [domburi](e2342.html).

The market is held a few steps from JR Hakodate Station.
