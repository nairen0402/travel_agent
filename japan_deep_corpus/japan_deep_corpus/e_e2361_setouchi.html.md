The **JR Setouchi Area Pass** is a [rail pass](e2357.html) for exclusive use by foreign tourists, providing seven consecutive days of unlimited travel on designated [JR](e2019.html) train lines (including [shinkansen](e2018.html)) and selected trams, [buses](e2015.html) and [ferries](e2355.html) in the region around the [Seto Inland Sea](e5445.html).

### Validity

1. Unlimited use of [local](e2016.html#category), [rapid](e2016.html#category) and [limited express](e2016.html#category) JR trains shown on the map above.
2. Unlimited use of the [Sanyo Shinkansen](e2018_sanyo.html) between Hakata and Shin-Osaka, including [Nozomi and Mizuho](e2018_sanyo.html) trains. Note that the [Tokaido Shinkansen](e2018_tokaido.html) between Shin-Osaka and [Kyoto](e2158.html) is not covered.
3. Seat reservations can be made for free (see details [below](#seat)).
4. The pass is valid on the following trams, [buses](e2015.html) and [ferries](e2355.html):
   * Trams in [Okayama](e5700.html)
   * Meipuru-pu tourist bus in [Hiroshima](e2160.html).
   * Buses between Okayama Station and Shin-Okayama Port
   * Ferries between Shin-Okayama Port and [Shodoshima](e5430.html)
   * Ferries between [Takamatsu](e5400.html) and [Shodoshima](e5430.html)'s Tonosho Port
   * Ferries between [Hiroshima](e2160.html) and [Matsuyama](e5500.html)
   * JR ferries to [Miyajima](e3401.html)
5. The pass is valid on seven consecutive days. The validity of the pass is based on calendar days (midnight to midnight) as opposed to 24-hour periods.
6. Only foreign visitors to Japan (on [temporary visitor](e2221.html) status) can use the pass. It cannot be used by residents of Japan.
7. The pass can be used only by a single person. It cannot be transferred between multiple persons. The pass holder has to carry his/her passport at all times.

### Points of sale

The JR Setouchi Area Pass can be purchased [online](https://affiliate.japan-experience.com/click?a=31&o=4&link_id=202) through various websites, including the [official JR West website](https://www.westjr.co.jp/global/en/), and at selected travel agents outside of Japan. The actual pass has to be picked up at a major station in the area covered before use. An advantage of purchasing the pass through the official website is that you can make [seat reservations](#seat) online, while others can make seat reservations only at ticket machines and ticket offices.

### Seat reservations

Seat reservations can be made at no additional cost at ticket offices. The first six reservations can alternatively be made at ticket machines, but subsequent reservations can be made only at ticket offices. Furthermore, those who purchased their pass through the [official JR West website](https://www.westjr.co.jp/global/en/) can also use the JR West website to make seat reservations, but the website is not very easy to use.

### Assessment

The JR Setouchi Area Pass can be a good value for those traveling along the [Sanyo Coast](/list/e1106.html) coupled with a visit to [Shikoku](/list/e1107.html) or [Shodoshima](e5430.html).

### Alternative tickets

There are a handful of other rail passes with slightly different coverage areas in the region. They include the [JR Sanyo Sanin Area Pass](e2361_sanyo_sanin.html), the [All Shikoku Rail Pass](e2357_shikoku.html), the [JR Kansai Hiroshima Area Pass](e2361_kansai_hiroshima.html), the [JR Sanin Okayama Area Pass](e2361_sanin.html), the [JR Kansai Wide Area Pass](e2361_08.html) and the [JR Hiroshima Yamaguchi Area Pass](e2361_hiroshima_yamaguchi.html). Before purchasing any, it is recommended to consider which one will be the most beneficial according to your itinerary.
