* 

  [Day 1 - Sapporo](e2163.html)

  Spend a full day in the capital of [Hokkaido](/list/e1101.html), visiting sites like the [Sapporo Beer Museum](e5300.html) and the night scene in [Susukino](e5305.html). Overnight in [Sapporo](e2163.html) for the next two nights. The [Sapporo Snow Festival](e5311.html) is held in early February.
* 

  [Day 2 - Day trip to Otaru](e6700.html)

  Make a sidetrip to [Otaru](e6700.html), about 40 minutes from [Sapporo](e2163.html) by train. The [canal area](e6701.html) is the main venue for the [Otaru Snow Light Path Festival](e6706.html) which takes place roughly at the same time as the [Sapporo Snow Festival](e5311.html).
* 

  [Day 3 and 4 - Niseko](e6720.html)

  Depart [Sapporo](e2163.html) for [Niseko](e6720.html) and enjoy the leading [snow resort](e2262_resort.html)'s slopes for two days.
* 

  [Day 5 - Niseko to Lake Toya](e6725.html)

  Take a bus to [Lake Toya](e6725.html) and stay overnight at Toyako Onsen, a [hot spring](e2292.html) resort town at the foot of [Mount Usu](e6726.html).
* 

  [Day 6 - Lake Toya to Noboribetsu](e6750.html)

  Not far from [Lake Toya](e6725.html) is [Noboribetsu](e6750.html), Hokkaido's most celebrated [hot spring resort](e2292.html). Enjoy the high quality waters and explore the water's source, the [hell valley](e6751.html).
* 

  [Day 7 - Noboribetsu to Asahikawa](e6890.html)

  Take the train to [Asahikawa](e6890.html) in the center of Hokkaido, famous for its popular [zoo](e6892.html) and [local ramen](e6893.html). The [Asahikawa Winter Festival](e6894.html) takes place around the same time as the [Sapporo Snow Festival](e5311.html).
* 

  [Day 8 - Asahikawa to Sounkyo](e6777.html)

  Proceed to [Sounkyo Onsen](e6777.html), a hot spring resort in [Daisetsuzan National Park](e6775.html). From late January to late March, the town holds the Sounkyo Ice Waterfall Festival.
* 

  [Day 9 - Sounkyo to Abashiri](e6865.html)

  Wake up early and make the relatively long journey to [Abashiri](e6865.html) on the eastern coast of [Hokkaido](/list/e1101.html). In the winter, [drift ice](e6868.html) can be seen in the sea off Abashiri.
* 

  [Day 10 and 11 - Shiretoko Peninsula](e6850.html)

  Travel from Abashiri to the nearby [Shiretoko Peninsula](e6850.html) and spend two nights in [Utoro](e6857.html). Enjoy the [drift ice](e6860.html) and walks through the winter landscape.
* 

  [Day 12 - Shiretoko to Sapporo](e6850.html)

  Travel to Memanbetsu Airport and fly back to [Sapporo](e2163.html) or [Tokyo](e2164.html).

The above itinerary is somewhat fast-paced. Tourists preferring a slow pace of travel should consider spending more time at some of the destinations along the way.
