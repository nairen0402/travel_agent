### Disney Hotels

The Disney Hotels are the most convenient and popular hotels for staying at Tokyo Disney Resort. Staying guests enjoy the benefits of Disney-themed rooms and amenities, complimentary monorail passes or shuttle bus rides to/from the parks, pre-check-in service at JR Maihama Station with baggage delivery to their room, complimentary shopping delivery to their room and purchase of park tickets even on days when tickets are sold out.

* 

  Tokyo DisneySea Hotel MiraCosta

  The Hotel MiraCosta stands partially inside [Tokyo DisneySea](e3016_sea.html) and offers views into the park from many of its rooms. Guests also enjoy access to a separate entrance from the hotel directly into the park and can enter the park a little bit earlier than regular visitors on certain days. The hotel facilities include themed restaurants, shops and spa facilities.
* 

  Tokyo Disneyland Hotel

  Opened in 2008, the Tokyo Disneyland Hotel stands just across from the entrance to Tokyo Disneyland and is designed in an early 20th Century Victorian style. Hotel facilities include themed gardens, restaurants, shops and an outdoor pool.

The **Tokyo DisneySea Hotel Fantasy Springs** opened in June 2024. Like the MiraCosta, it comes with rooms looking into the park, while staying guests enjoy privileges, such as an exclusive entrance from the hotel directly into the park. The **Toy Story Hotel** opened in 2022 next to Bayside Station along the monorail. Themed after the popular movie series, it caters to travelers on a moderate budget. Lastly, the **Tokyo Disney Celebration Hotel**, is the only one located outside of the Disney Resort grounds, about a 15 minute shuttle bus away. It caters to travelers with a somewhat lower budget.

### Official Hotels

The six Official Hotels are located on the resort grounds near Bayside Station of the monorail line. Staying guests enjoy the benefits of complimentary shuttle bus service between their hotel and Bayside Station, complimentary baggage delivery to their hotel from JR Maihama Station and purchase of tickets even on days when tickets are sold out.

### Partner Hotels

Four Partner Hotels are located off the park grounds, just a short drive from the resort. Guests of partner hotels enjoy the benefit of complimentary shuttle bus service to and from the resort and can have their baggage delivered to their hotel from JR Maihama Station for a small fee. The benefit of purchasing park tickets is currenlty not available.

* [Hotel Emion Tokyo Bay](https://www.booking.com/hotel/jp/emion-tokyo-bay.html?aid=355588)
* [Oriental Hotel Tokyo Bay](https://www.booking.com/hotel/jp/oriental-tokyo-bay.html?aid=355588)
* [Urayasu Brighton Hotel Tokyo Bay](https://www.booking.com/hotel/jp/urayasu-brighton-tokyo-bay.html?aid=355588)
* [Mitsui Garden Hotel Prana Tokyo Bay](https://www.agoda.com/mitsui-garden-hotel-prana-tokyo-bay/hotel/tokyo-jp.html?site_id=25544)
