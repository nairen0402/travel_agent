Hachimantai (八幡平) is a mountainous region in northern [Akita](/list/e1203.html) and [Iwate](/list/e1204.html) Prefectures, which makes up the southern part of the [Towada-Hachimantai National Park](e3658.html). The region is far removed from Japan's [cities](/list/e1003.html) and offers some of the country's best rustic [hot springs](e2292.html) and [volcanic landscapes](e2327.html) that will delight travelers looking to explore the Japanese countryside.
