Hakone (箱根) is part of the Fuji-Hakone-Izu National Park, less than one hundred kilometers from [Tokyo](e2164.html). Famous for [hot springs](e5209.html), natural beauty and the view across [Lake Ashinoko](e5201.html) of nearby [Mount Fuji](e2172.html), Hakone is one of the most popular destinations among Japanese and international tourists looking for a break from [Tokyo](e2164.html).
