Take the JR [Tohoku](e2018_tohoku.html)/[Hokkaido](e2018_hokkaido.html) Shinkansen from [Tokyo](e2164.html) to Shin-Hakodate-Hokuto (4 hours) and transfer to the Hokuto limited express to Noboribetsu Station (2.5 hours). The entire one way trip costs around 28,000 yen and takes around seven hours. It is fully covered by the [Japan Rail Pass](e2361.html) and [JR East South Hokkaido Rail Pass](e2361_southhokkaido.html). Note that seat reservations are mandatory on [Hayabusa](e2018_tohoku.html) trains between Tokyo and Shin-Hakodate-Hokuto and on Hokuto limited express trains.

Above fees and schedules are subject to change. Be sure to check current yen [exchange rates](e2196.html).

JR Noboribetsu Station is located in the city's urban center close to the coast, while the [hot spring town](e6752.html) and [Jigokudani](e6751.html) are a 15 minute [car](e2024.html), [taxi](e2021.html) or [bus](e2015.html) ride from the station inland into the hills. A taxi ride costs around 3000 yen, while a bus ride costs 450 yen one way. There are about one to two buses per hour.
