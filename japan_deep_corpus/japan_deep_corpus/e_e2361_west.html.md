The **JR West All Area Pass** is a [rail pass](e2357.html) for exclusive use by foreign tourists, providing seven consecutive days of unlimited travel on designated [JR](e2019.html) train and bus lines (including [shinkansen](e2018.html) and [limited express](e2016.html) trains) in the entire service area of [JR West](e2019.html) which includes the [Kansai](/list/e1105.html), [Chugoku](/list/e1106.html) and Hokuriku regions.

### Validity

1. Unlimited use of [local](e2016.html), [rapid](e2016.html) and [limited express](e2016.html) JR trains in the coverage area shown on the map above, including trains along the Chizukyu Express Line between Kamigori and Chizu, a non-JR line that provides the fastest train access to [Tottori](e8100.html).
2. Unlimited use of the [Sanyo Shinkansen](e2018_sanyo.html) (including Nozomi and Mizuho) between Shin-Osaka and [Fukuoka](e2161.html)'s Hakata Station and of the [Hokuriku Shinkansen](e2018_nagano.html) between [Tsuruga](e6650.html) and Joetsu-Myoko Station. However, the [Tokaido Shinkansen](e2018_tokaido.html) between [Kyoto](e2158.html) and [Osaka](e2157.html) is not covered.
3. Seat reservations can be made for free (see details [below](#seat)).
4. Unlimited use of the IR Ishikawa Railway between [Kanazawa](e2167.html) and Tsubata to access the JR Nanao Line, on the Ainokaze Toyama Railway between [Toyama](e7500.html) and [Takaoka](e7525.html) to access the JR Himi and Johana lines, and on the Hapi Line Fukui between [Fukui](e6600.html) and Echizen-Hanando to access the JR Kuzuryu Line; however, it is not allowed to get on and off at any stations in between. The liner surcharge for Ainokaze Liner trains is not covered.
5. Unlimited use of the Kyoto Tango Railway, which provides access to [Amanohashidate](e3990.html).
6. Unlimited use of JR ferry to [Miyajima](e3401.html).
7. Unlimited use of local JR buses around [Kyoto](e2158.html), [Kanazawa](e2167.html), [Hiroshima](e2161.html) and [Yamaguchi](e6100.html), including buses between [Kyoto Station](e3922.html), [Takao](e3940.html) and Shuzan, and between Yamaguchi, [Hagi](e6150.html) and [Akiyoshidai](e6130.html).
8. Free use of rental bicycles at designated Ekirin Kun outlets within the covered area.
9. The pass is valid on seven consecutive calendar days (midnight to midnight).
10. Only foreign visitors to Japan (with [temporary visitor](e2221.html) status) can use the pass. It cannot be used by residents of Japan.
11. The pass can be used only by a single person. It cannot be transferred between multiple persons. The pass holder has to carry his/her passport at all times.

### Points of sale

The JR West All Area Pass can be purchased [online](https://affiliate.japan-experience.com/click?o=4&a=31&link_id=831) through various websites, including the [official JR West website](https://www.westjr.co.jp/global/en/), and at selected travel agents outside of Japan. The actual pass has to be picked up at a major station in the area covered before use. An advantage of purchasing the pass through the official website is that you can make [seat reservations](#seat) online, while others can make seat reservations only at ticket machines and ticket offices.

### Seat reservations

Seat reservations can be made at no additional cost at ticket offices. The first six reservations can alternatively be made at ticket machines, but subsequent reservations can be made only at ticket offices. Furthermore, those who purchased their pass through the [official JR West website](https://www.westjr.co.jp/global/en/) can also use the JR West website to make seat reservations, but the website is not very easy to use.

### Assessment

The JR West All Area Pass can be a very good value for those visiting all three regions covered, namely the [Kansai](/list/e1105.html), [Chugoku](/list/e1106.html) and Hokuriku regions.
