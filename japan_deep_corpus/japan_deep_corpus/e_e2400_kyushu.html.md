* 

  [Day 1 - Fukuoka](e2161.html)

  Explore [Fukuoka](e2161.html) city and check out the city's famous [food stalls](e4803.html) (yatai) in the evening.
* 

  [Day 2 - Fukuoka to Nagasaki](e2162.html)

  Travel to [Nagasaki](e2162.html) and explore the city. Stay overnight there.
* 

  [Day 3 - Nagasaki to Kagoshima](e4600.html)

  Leave Nagasaki early and head to [Kagoshima](e4600.html) in southern [Kyushu](/list/e1108.html). Check out [Sakurajima](e4601.html), an active volcano and the symbol of Kagoshima.
* 

  [Day 4 - Kagoshima to Yakushima](e4650.html)

  Board the [high speed ferry](e2355.html) from [Kagoshima](e4600.html) to [Yakushima](e4650.html). A rental car makes it easier to explore the island. Spend two nights on Yakushima.
* 

  [Day 5 and 6 - Yakushima](e4650.html)

  Spend an entire day exploring [Yakushima](e4650.html) before taking the ferry back to [Kagoshima](e4600.html) on day 6. Overnight in Kagoshima.
* 

  [Day 7 - Kagoshima to Kirishima](e4625.html)

  Explore the [Kirishima Mountains](e4625.html) and overnight in [Kirishima Onsen](e4628.html).
* 

  [Day 8 - Kirishima to Kumamoto](e4500.html)

  Go to [Kumamoto](e4500.html), explore the city and visit [Kumamoto Castle](e4501.html).
* 

  [Day 9 - Kumamoto to Takachiho](e8050.html)

  Explore the town of [Takachiho](e8050.html) and attend a Yokagura performance at [Takachiho Shrine](e8053.html) in the evening. Overnight in Takachiho.
* 

  [Day 10 - Takachiho to Kurokawa via Aso](e4550.html)

  Visit [Mount Aso](e4552.html) along your way from Takachiho to [Kurokawa Onsen](e4575.html). Stay at a [ryokan](e2029.html) in Kurokawa.
* 

  [Day 11 - Yufuin via Mount Kuju](e4750.html)

  Move on to [Yufuin](e4750.html) via the [Kuju Mountains](e4775.html) and overnight in Yufuin.
* 

  [Day 12 and 13 - Beppu](e4700.html)

  Head to [Beppu](e4700.html) and visit the town's [hot springs](e4701.html). Stay in Beppu and return to [Fukuoka](e2161.html) via [Kitakyushu](e4875.html) the next day.

The above itinerary is somewhat fast-paced. Tourists preferring a slow pace of travel should consider spending more time at some of the destinations along the way.
