Kappabashi Street (かっぱ橋道具街, Kappabashi Dōgugai) is a shopping street between Ueno and [Asakusa](e3004.html), which is lined with several dozens of stores selling everything needed by [restaurant](e2036.html) operators, with the exception of fresh [food](e620.html).

You will find specialized stores for dishes, pots, pans, cooking utensils, stoves, tables, chairs, signs, lanterns and more. There are also a few stores which sell plastic and wax food samples, used by many [restaurants](e2036.html) in their show windows.

Plastic food samples

The closest station is Tawaramachi Station on the Ginza Subway Line, a short walk away. It can also be reached from [Asakusa](e3004.html)'s [Sensoji Temple](e3001.html) in about a 10 minute walk.
