The **JR East Nagano Niigata Area Pass** is a [rail pass](e2357.html) for exclusive use by foreigners in Japan, providing unlimited travel on [JR](e2019.html) trains (including [shinkansen](e2018.html) and [limited express](e2016.html#category) trains) and local JR [buses](e2015.html) in the [Greater Tokyo](/list/e1103.html) area, in [Nagano](/list/e1216.html) and [Niigata](/list/e1207.html) prefectures and in the Shonai Region of [Yamagata Prefecture](/list/e1205.html) on five consecutive days.

### Validity

1. Unlimited use of trains operated by [JR East](e2019.html) in the [Kanto](/list/e1103.html) (Tokyo, Kanagawa, Chiba, Saitama, Gunma, Tochigi and Ibaraki prefectures) and Koshinetsu (Yamanashi, Nagano and Niigata prefectures) regions plus the Shonai Region of [Yamagata Prefecture](/list/e1205.html) and the [Izu Peninsula](e6310.html). The pass is also valid on the Tokyo Monorail between central Tokyo and [Haneda Airport](e2430.html).
2. In the designated area, you can use all types of JR East trains, including [shinkansen](e2018.html) (except [Tokaido Shinkansen](e2018_tokaido.html), which is operated by JR Central), [limited express](e2016.html#category) trains and local trains (except some "liner" trains). The [Hokuriku Shinkansen](e2018_nagano.html) is covered as far as Joetsu Myoko Station.
3. Seat reservations can be made for free (see details [below](#seat)).
4. There are a small number of JR trains, which partially run on the tracks of different railway companies. When riding along a [non-JR owned section](e2368.html), pass holders will have to pay the fare for the non-JR section on board of the train or at the station. Only the following non-JR train lines can also be used with the JR East Nagano Niigata Area Pass:
   * Limited express trains between [Shinjuku](e3011.html) and Tobu-[Nikko](e3800.html) and [Kinugawa Onsen](e3875.html) and local trains between Tobu-Nikko, Shimo-Imaichi and Kinugawa Onsen
   * All trains along the Izukyu Line between Ito and Izukyu-[Shimoda](e6300.html) on the [Izu Peninsula](e6310.html)
   * All trains along the Hokuhoku Line between Echigo-[Yuzawa](e7690.html) and Naoetsu
   * All trains between Naoetsu and Arai on the Echigo Tokimeki Railway
   * Tokyo Monorail between central Tokyo and [Haneda Airport](e2430.html)
5. Unlimited use of local JR [buses](e2015.html) (but not [highway buses](e2366.html)) in the coverage area. Some of the more useful lines for tourists are:
   * Buses from Naganohara-Kusatsuguchi Station to [Kusatsu Onsen](e7400.html)
   * Buses from Nasu-Shiobara Station to [Shiobara Onsen](e3843.html)
   * Buses from [Utsunomiya](e3860.html) to Twin Ring Motegi
   * Buses from Chino Station and Ina to [Takato Castle](e6065.html) (seasonal)
   * Buses from Sakudaira Station to Takamine Kogen
   * Buses from [Tateyama](e6434.html) to around the southern tip of the [Boso Peninsula](e6430.html)
   * Buses between Yokokawa Station and [Karuizawa](e6030.html)
6. The pass is valid on five consecutive calendar days (midnight to midnight).
7. The pass can be purchased and used only by holders of non-Japanese passports, including foreign residents of Japan.

### Points of sale

The JR East Nagano Niigata Area Pass can be purchased [online](https://affiliate.japan-experience.com/click?o=4&a=31&link_id=871) through various websites, through selected travel agents outside of Japan and at major railway stations and airports in the area covered. If purchasing the pass outside of Japan or via the internet, the actual pass has to be picked up at a ticket office of a major station in the area covered before use.

Note that the pass is also sold at the [official JR East website](https://www.jreast.co.jp/multi/en/). An advantage of purchasing through the official website is that you can pick up your pass also at ticket machines that are equipped with passport readers. Purchasing passes inside Japan is also possible at these passport reader-equipped ticket machines.

### Seat reservations

Free seat reservations can be made at seat reservation ticket machines and at JR ticket offices. It is also possible to make online seat reservations on the [JR East Train Reservation website](https://www.eki-net.com/en/jreast-train-reservation/Top/Index); however, physical seat reservation tickets have to be picked up at a selected ticket machine or office before use.

### Assessment

The JR East Nagano Niigata Area Pass can be a good value for those visiting [Nagano](/list/e1216.html), [Niigata](/list/e1207.html) or the Shonai Region of [Yamagata Prefecture](/list/e1205.html) (e.g. [Dewa Sanzan](e7900.html)) from [Tokyo](e2164.html). Alternatively, it can pay off for making multiple daytrips from Tokyo to various destinations in the coverage area.
