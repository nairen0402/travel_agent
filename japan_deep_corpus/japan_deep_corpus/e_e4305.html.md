Ninja Kingdom Ise (伊勢忍者キングダム, Ise Ninja Kingdom) is a [theme park](e2067.html) about the [Azuchi Momoyama Period](e2123.html) of Japanese history, when the [samurai](e2127.html) ruled the country.

The theme park is built like a small [castle town](e2296.html) at the foot of a 1:1 replica of the Azuchi Castle (modern on the inside), seat of [Oda Nobunaga](e2123.html), one of Japan's most important historical figures. The castle town consists of various buildings and areas such as a [ninja](e2295.html) museum, ninja maze, ninja adventure playground, sword gallery, theater venues and various restaurants. In addition, a public [hot spring](e2292.html) bath (currently closed for renovations) is located next to the park's entrance.

Visitors can rent costumes to wear in the park

The Ninja Kingdom Ise can be reached in a 15 minute walk from JR Futaminoura Station (10 minutes, 210 yen, hourly departures from Iseshi Station). Alternatively, the theme park is also served by the [CAN bus](e4304.html).
