Beppu (別府) is one of Japan's most famous [hot spring](e2292.html) resorts, producing more hot spring water than any other resort in the country.

Beppu offers an unmatched range of [baths](e4701.html) to be enjoyed, including ordinary hot water baths, mud baths, sand baths and steam baths. In addition, the [Hells of Beppu](e4702.html) are several spectacular hot springs for viewing rather than bathing.
