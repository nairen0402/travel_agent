Kotohira (琴平) is a small town in [Kagawa Prefecture](/list/e1233.html), famous for [Kompirasan](e5451.html), [Shikoku](/list/e1107.html)'s most popular [shrine](e2059.html). Kompirasan is dedicated to seafaring, and is said to have one of the more challenging shrine approaches in Japan. Nearby is the historical [Kanamaruza](e5452.html) kabuki theater.
