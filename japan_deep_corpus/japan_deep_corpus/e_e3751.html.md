JAL operates multiple [flights](e2365.html) per day between [Tokyo](e2164.html)'s [Haneda Airport](e2430.html) and Aomori Airport. Flight duration is 80 minutes, and the regular one way fare is about 41,000 yen; however, [discount tickets](e2364.html) are usually available for around 16,000 yen. From the airport JR [buses](e2015.html) travel to JR Aomori Station (35 minutes, 860 yen one way, covered by the [Japan Rail Pass](e2361.html) but not by any other JR passes).

Take a Hayabusa train on the [JR Tohoku Shinkansen](e2018_tohoku.html) from [Tokyo](e2164.html) to Shin-Aomori (3.5 hours) and transfer to a local or limited express train for Aomori Station (5 minutes). The entire one way trip takes around four hours, costs about 18,000 yen and is fully covered by the [Japan Rail Pass](e2361.html), [JR East Tohoku Area Pass](e2361_tohoku.html) and [JR East South Hokkaido Pass](e2361_southhokkaido.html). Seat reservations are mandatory on Hayabusa trains. There is about one connection per hour.

Konan Bus and several other companies operates overnight [highway buses](e2366.html) between [Tokyo](e2164.html) and Aomori. The one way journey takes around 11 hours and costs between 4,500 and 11,000 yen depending on company, bus type and date of travel. Online reservations can be made for some of the buses through [Willer](https://willerexpress.com/en/bus_search/tokyo/all/aomori/aomori/?utm_source=web&utm_medium=ad&utm_campaign=af01&aid=184) and [Japan Bus Online](https://japanbusonline.com/en/AreaSearch2/3130000/2022012/?afcd=MDI=).

**Aomori Station** is located next to Aomori Port in the city center. Most of the city's attractions are within walking distance from the station. A bus network, centered at Aomori Station, provides access around the city and to the less centrally located [Aomori Museum of Art](e3752.html) and [Sannai Maruyama Jomon Sites](e3753.html). Buses also travel to destinations further away such as [Mount Hakkoda](e3780.html) and [Lake Towada](e3776.html).

The [shinkansen](e2018.html) station, **Shin-Aomori Station**, is located a 6-7 minute train ride from Aomori Station in the outskirts of the city. [Local](e2016.html) and [limited express](e2016.html) trains operate relatively frequently between the two stations, and a special rule allows travelers to use limited express trains without a [limited express supplement](e2359_001.html) when riding between just the two stations in the unreserved section of the train.

**Aomori Airport** is connected by a JR bus line with JR Aomori Station (35 minutes, 860 yen one way, fully covered by the [Japan Rail Pass](e2361.html) and [JR East Tohoku Area Pass](e2361_tohoku.html), but not by the [JR East South Hokkaido Pass](e2361_southhokkaido.html) and [JR Tohoku South Hokkaido Rail Pass](e2361_tohoku_hokkaido.html)).
