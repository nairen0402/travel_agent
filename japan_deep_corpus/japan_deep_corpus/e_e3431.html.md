Fukuyama is a station along the [JR Sanyo Shinkansen](e2018_sanyo.html). Direct [shinkansen](e2018.html) trains connect Fukuyama Station to Shin-Osaka (about one hour, 7,500 yen one way), Hiroshima (about 25 minutes, 4,500 yen one way) and Tokyo (about 3.5 hours, 17,000 yen one way) among others. The [Japan Rail Pass](e2361.html) fully covers [Sakura, Hikari and Kodama](e2018_sanyo.html) trains, but a supplement fee is required on [Nozomi and Mizuho](e2018_sanyo.html) trains.

Above fees and schedules are subject to change. Be sure to check current yen [exchange rates](e2196.html).

Fukuyama Station is the main transportation hub of the city and is served by the [shinkansen](e2018.html), local trains and [buses](e2015.html). [Fukuyama Castle](e3433.html) stands immediately north of the station, while [Tomonoura](e3432.html) and [Shinshoji Temple](e3434.html) are on the rural peninsula south of the city center and can be accessed by bus. More detailed access information can be found on their respective pages.
