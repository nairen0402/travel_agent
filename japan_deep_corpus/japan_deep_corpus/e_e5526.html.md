### How travel between Ozu and Matsuyama

See [how to get to Matsuyama](e5504.html)

#### By train

The one way journey between [Matsuyama](e5500.html) and [Ozu](e5525.html) (Iyo-Ozu Station) by [limited express train](e2016.html#category) takes about 40 minutes and costs 1840 yen by unreserved seat or around 2500 yen by reserved seat. By [local train](e2016.html#category), the journey takes 90-110 minutes, but costs only 1080 yen. Limited express trains depart once per hour whereas local trains depart once every 1-2 hours. All trains are covered by the [Japan Rail Pass](e2361.html).

#### By bus

Uwajima Bus operates [buses](e2015.html) once every 1-2 hours between Matsuyama-shi Station and Iyo-Ozu Station. The one way journey takes about one hour and costs 1250 yen.

Above fees and schedules are subject to change. Be sure to check current yen [exchange rates](/e/e2196.html).

[Ozu](e5525.html)'s main [railway](e2019.html) station is called **Iyo-Ozu Station**. It is located about one kilometer north of the [castle](e5527.html) and [old town](e5529.html). The attractions of Ozu are located close to each other, and good walkers can easily explore them entirely on foot. Both the castle and the old town are about a 20 minute walk from Iyo-Ozu Station, while the [Garyu Sanso Villa](e5528.html) is a further 5-10 minute walk to the eastern fringe of the old town.

Alternatively, [rental bicycles](e2210.html) are available at the visitor information center at the station for 300 yen per hour or 1000 yen per day.
