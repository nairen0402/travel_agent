Below is a list of [Kumamoto](e4500.html)'s most popular spots for cherry blossom viewing ([hanami](e2011.html)), including the typical timing for best viewing in the average year and japan-guide.com ratings. See also our nationwide list of [famous cherry blossom spots](e2011_where.html).

* 

  [Kumamoto Castle••](e4501.html)

  Late March to early April

  10 minute tram ride from Kumamoto Station

  Despite the fact that its inner grounds are currently closed to the public due to repair works, [Kumamoto Castle](e4501.html) remains the city's most popular cherry blossom spot. Nearly a thousand cherry trees are planted across the castle grounds and in the adjacent public parks.
* 

  [Suizenji Koen•](e4502.html)

  Late March to early April

  30 minute tram ride from Kumamoto Station  
  8:30 to 17:00 (entry until 16:30), 400 yen

  About 150 cherry trees stand in [Suizenji Koen](e4502.html), a beautiful [Japanese garden](e2099.html) with central pond and a grass covered, miniature replica of [Mount Fuji](e2172.html). Picnicking is allowed in designated areas of the park.
