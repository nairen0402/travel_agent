Abashiri (網走) is a city of just over 30,000 people on the eastern coast of [Hokkaido](/list/e1101.html), not far from [Shiretoko](e6850.html) and [Akan](e6800.html) national parks. The city's main tourist attractions are its [prison museum](e6867.html) and the [drift ice](e6868.html), which can be observed in the Okhotsk Sea during winter.
