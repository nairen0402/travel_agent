Abashiri has a longish city center without prominent landmarks. JR Abashiri Station stands at its western end. The [Prison Museum](e6867.html), [Ryuhyo Museum](e6868.html) and [Museum of Northern Peoples](e6869.html) are located on nearby Mount Tento, a 5-10 minute bus or car ride from the city center.

**Memanbetsu Airport** is located approximately 20 kilometers southwest of Abashiri. The [bus](e2015.html) trip from the airport to JR Abashiri Station and Abashiri Bus Terminal takes about 30 minutes and costs 1050 yen one way. Various [rental car](e2024.html) companies have outlets at the airport.

A **tourist bus** connects Abashiri Bus Center with JR Abashiri Station and the museums on Mount Tento. In winter, it also serves the [ryuhyo cruise boat](e6868.html) terminal. The buses operates daily every 60-90 minutes during the busier travel seasons, less frequent in the shoulder seasons and not at all on weekdays in the off-seasons. Fares are between 180 and 530 yen, depending on the distance traveled.
