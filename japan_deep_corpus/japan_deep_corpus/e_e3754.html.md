Auga Market (アウガ新鮮市場, Auga Shinsen Ichiba) is a lively [fish market](e2452.html) located in the basement of the Auga Festival City shopping center in central [Aomori City](e3750.html). The market consists of tightly packed rows of vendors selling all sorts of seafood, vegetables and prepared foods.

The local seafood is freshly brought in from Aomori Port, and you can find practically anything here from seaweed and tuna, to live octopus and Aomori's famous scallops. There are also a few restaurants that serve seafood from the market.

The Auga Fish Market is located in the basement of the Auga Festival City shopping center, one block from JR Aomori Station.
