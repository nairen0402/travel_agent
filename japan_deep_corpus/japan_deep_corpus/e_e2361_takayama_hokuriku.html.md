The **Takayama-Hokuriku Area Tourist Pass** is a [rail pass](e2357.html) for exclusive use by foreign tourists. It provides pass holders with five consecutive days of unlimited travel on JR trains connecting [Nagoya](e2155.html) with [Takayama](e5900.html) and [Toyama](e7500.html), and [Osaka](e2157.html) with [Kanazawa](e2167.html) and Toyama, as well as on buses to [Shirakawago](e5950.html).

### Validity

The map below shows the train and bus lines covered by the pass:

1. Unlimited use of JR [limited express](e2016.html#category) and [local](e2016.html#category) trains between [Nagoya](e2155.html), [Takayama](e5900.html) and [Toyama](e7500.html).
     
   Unlimited use of JR limited express and local trains between [Osaka](e2157.html) and [Tsuruga](e6650.html).
     
   Unlimited use of the [JR Hokuriku Shinkansen](e2018_nagano.html) between Tsuruga and Toyama.
     
   Unlimited use of JR trains in central Osaka.
     
   Unlimited use of JR trains between Osaka and [Kansai Airport](e2033.html).
     
   Unlimited use of [buses between Takayama, Toyama, Takaoka and Kanazawa](/bus/shirakawago.html).
     
   Unlimited use of [buses between Takaoka, Gokayama and Shirakawago](/bus/gokayama.html).
2. Pass holders can make up to six seat reservations for trains ([limited express](e2016.html#category) and [shinkansen](e2018.html)) for free.
3. Most [buses between Takayama, Toyama, Takaoka and Kanazawa](/bus/shirakawago.html) require seat reservations. They are free but should be made in advance by phone or at the bus terminal.
4. The pass is valid on five consecutive days. The validity of the passes is based on calendar days (midnight to midnight) as opposed to 24-hour periods.
5. Only foreign visitors to Japan (on [temporary visitor](e2221.html) status) can use the pass. It cannot be used by residents of Japan.
6. The pass can be used only by a single person. It cannot be transferred between multiple persons. The pass holder has to carry his/her passport at all times.

### Assessment

The Takayama-Hokuriku Area Tourist Pass can provide savings and convenience for travelers interested in visiting [Takayama](e5900.html), [Shirakawago](e5950.html) and [Kanazawa](e2167.html) from [Nagoya](e2155.html), [Kyoto](e2158.html) or [Osaka](e2157.html). Other destinations accessible with this pass include [Gero Onsen](e5990.html), [Kaga Onsen](e4275.html) and [Fukui](e6600.html).

### Alternative tickets

The [Shoryudo Bus Pass](e2357_shoryudo.html) covers a similar area as the Takayama-Hokuriku Area Tourist Pass (except the Kanazawa-Osaka section which is not covered), but can be used only on buses.
