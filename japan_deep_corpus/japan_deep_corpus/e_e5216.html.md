Gora Park (強羅公園, Gōra Kōen) is a Western-style landscape park located on the steep slope above Gora Station. It is a relaxing place to unwind and enjoy the scenery and views of [Hakone](e5200.html).

Gora Park is primarily a French styled landscape park featuring a large fountain and a rose garden. The park also has two greenhouses, one housing a tropical botanical garden while the other contains a [flower](e2423.html) garden. Additionally, there is a restaurant overlooking the main fountain, as well as the Hakuun-do Chaen teahouse.

In the Crafthouse visitors can take part in [craft activities](e2424.html) such as glass blowing, glass etching, [pottery](e2329.html) and dried flower arrangement. Activities range in cost from 1000 to 5000 yen and take from 30 minutes to an hour to complete.

Gora Park is a five minute walk from Gora Station, the terminal station of [Hakone Tozan Railway](e5202.html). It takes about 35 minutes from Hakone-Yumoto to Gora Station, costs 460 yen, and there are trains every 15-20 minutes.
