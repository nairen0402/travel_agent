Noboribetsu Onsen (登別温泉) is [Hokkaido](/list/e1101.html)'s most famous [hot spring](e2292.html) resort. A large amount of Noboribetsu's many types of hot spring water surfaces in the spectacular [Jigokudani](e6751.html) or "Hell Valley" just above the resort town. Noboribetsu is part of [Shikotsu-Toya National Park](e6735.html).
