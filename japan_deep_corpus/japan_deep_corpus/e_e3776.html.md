Towada (十和田) is a city in [Aomori Prefecture](/list/e1202.html) most famous for [Lake Towada](e3778.html), the largest caldera lake on Honshu, Japan's main island. Part of [Towada-Hachimantai National Park](e3658.html), the area is well known for its [autumn colors](e2014.html) especially around the [Oirase Stream](e3775.html), one of Japan's most famous autumn color spots.
