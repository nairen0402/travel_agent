ANA and JTA (Japan Transocean Air), a member of the JAL Group, operate direct [flights](e2365.html) between [Tokyo](e2164.html)'s [Haneda Airport](e2430.html) and Ishigaki Airport on [Ishigaki Island](e7201.html), the transportation hub of the [Yaeyama Islands](e7200.html), while Peach Aviation connects the island with Tokyo's [Narita Airport](e2027.html). Flight duration is around three hours. The regular one way fare by ANA and JTA is about 77,000 yen, while [discount fares](e2364.html) are usually available for between 25,000 and 45,000 yen. The fares by Peach Aviation tend to be much lower, starting around 8000 yen one way.
