JAL, ANA, Solaseed and Jetstar Japan operate frequent [flights](e2365.html) between [Tokyo](e2164.html) (mostly [Haneda Airport](e2430.html), except Peach and Jetstar which use [Narita Airport](e2027.html)) and Oita Airport near [Beppu](e4700.html). Flight duration is 90 minutes. The regular one way fare by JAL and ANA is about 48,000 yen, but [discount tickets](e2364.html) are usually available for around 17,000 to 25,000 yen. Fares by Solaseed Air are typically 20 percent lower than those by JAL and ANA, while the fares by Jetstar start from around 7,000 yen.

The one way [bus](e2015.html) journey between Oita Airport and Beppu takes 50 minutes and costs 1600 yen. All buses stop at Beppu Kitahama, and a few buses also serve JR Beppu Station. Several [rental car](e2024.html) companies have an outlet at Oita Airport.

Take the [Tokaido/Sanyo Shinkansen](e2018.html) from [Tokyo](e2164.html) to Kokura Station and transfer to the Sonic limited express train for [Beppu](e4700.html). The entire one way trip takes about 6 hours and costs around 26,000 yen. The [Japan Rail Pass](e2361.html) fully covers the trip if done by the slower [Hikari](e2018_tokaido.html) and [Sakura](e2018_sanyo.html) trains, but a supplement fee is required on the [Nozomi](e2018_sanyo.html) and [Mizuho](e2018_sanyo.html) trains.

Above fees and schedules are subject to change. Be sure to check current yen [exchange rates](e2196.html).

[Beppu](e4700.html)'s attractions are spread all across the city and are best explored by [rental car](e2024.html) or [bus](e2015.html). The city's bus network is operated by two companies: Kamenoi Bus and Oita Kotsu.

Kamenoi Bus provides access to most places in Beppu except the [Takasakiyama Monkey Park](e4703.html) and the [Umitamago Aquarium](e4704.html). Kamenoi also offers practical 1-day and 2-day unlimited bus passes (1100 and 1700 yen respectively) that cover all of the sites within central Beppu. Special wide versions of the passes (1800 and 2800 yen) include the buses to [Yufuin](e4750.html). Oita Kotsu operates buses to the monkey park, the aquarium and to Oita Airport.

[Highway buses](e2366.html) and most airport buses stop at the Kitahama bus terminal, which can be reached from Beppu Station in about a 5-10 minute walk. Beppu Port, with [ferry](e2355.html) connections to [Shikoku](/list/e1107.html) and [Osaka](e2157.html), can be reached in a ten minute bus ride from Beppu Station or the Kitahama bus terminal.

The closest [airport](e2365.html) to Beppu is Oita Airport. Buses connect the airport with Beppu's Kitahama bus terminal in 50 minutes and for 1600 yen one way.
