* 

  [Day 1-6 Tokyo](e2164.html)

  Stay six nights in the city of [Tokyo](e2164.html) and go to Nikko, Kamakura and Yokohama on three day trips.
* 

  [Side trip - Nikko](e3806.html)

  Visit [Nikko](e3800.html), home to [Toshogu](e3801.html), Japan's most lavishly decorated shrine and the mausoleum of Tokugawa Ieyasu, the founder of the Tokugawa shogunate.
* 

  [Side trip - Kamakura](e2166.html)

  [Kamakura](e2166.html) used to be the political center of Japan in the 1200s. There are numerous temples, shrines and historical monuments to see all year, as well as [sand beaches](e3109.html) to go to in the summer.
* 

  [Side trip - Yokohama](e2156.html)

  [Yokohama](e2156.html) is about 30 minutes away from [Tokyo](e2164.html), and is also Japan's second largest city. The port area is very lively with many malls, an amusement park, museums and one of the world's largest [Chinatowns](e3201.html) located nearby.
* 

  [Day 7 - Tokyo to Hakone](e5200.html)

  Travel to [Hakone](e5200.html) and spend the night there. The area is known for its [hot springs](e5209.html), museums and the view of nearby [Mt. Fuji](e2172.html).
* 

  [Day 8 - Hakone to Fuji Five Lakes](e6900.html)

  Head to the [Fuji Five Lakes](e6900.html) area at the northern base of [Mt. Fuji](e2172.html). There are plenty of hot springs and museums, and [Fuji Q Highland](e6918.html), one of Japan's most popular [amusement parks](e2067.html). Overnight in the Fuji Five Lakes area.
* 

  [Day 9 - Fuji Five Lakes to Matsumoto](e6050.html)

  Leave the [Fuji Five Lakes](e6900.html) and head to [Matsumoto](e6050.html) in [Nagano Prefecture](/list/e1216.html), home to one of Japan's most beautiful original [castles](e2296.html), [Matsumotojo](e6051.html). Overnight in Matsumoto.
* 

  [Day 10 - Matsumoto to Yamanouchi](e6025.html)

  Head to Yamanouchi to see monkeys bathing in a [natural hot spring](e2292.html) at the [Jigokudani Monkey Park](e6028.html). Overnight at a [ryokan](e2029.html) in one of [Yamanouchi](e6025.html) onsen towns.
* 

  [Day 11 - Yamanouchi to Kusatsu](e7400.html)

  Travel through the mountains to [Kusatsu Onsen](e7400.html) in [Gunma Prefecture](/list/e1209.html), one of Japan's [best hot spring resorts](e2292_where.html). Overnight in Kusatsu. Note that shortest route between Yamanouchi and Kusatsu is closed during the winter.
* 

  [Day 12 - Kusatsu to Tokyo via Karuizawa](e6030.html)

  Enjoy a last dip in [Kusatsu](e7400.html)'s high quality [hot spring](e2292.html) water before returning to Tokyo. There is an option to drop by [Karuizawa](e6030.html), an upmarket mountain resort, along the way.

The above itinerary is somewhat fast-paced. Tourists preferring a slow pace of travel should consider spending more time at some of the destinations along the way.
