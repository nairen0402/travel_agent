* 

  [Day 1 and 2 - Tokyo](e2164.html)

  Spend three nights in [Tokyo](e2164.html). Consider our suggested [Tokyo itineraries](e3051.html).
* 

  [Day 3 - Side trip to Nikko](e3806.html)

  In a side trip from Tokyo, visit [Nikko](e3800.html), home to [Toshogu](e3801.html), Japan's most lavishly decorated shrine and the mausoleum of Tokugawa Ieyasu, the founder of the Tokugawa shogunate.
* 

  [Day 4 - Tokyo to Hakone](e5200.html)

  Travel to [Hakone](e5200.html) and spend the night there. The area is known for its [hot springs](e5209.html), museums and the view of nearby [Mt. Fuji](e2172.html).
* 

  [Day 5 to 7 - Kyoto](e2158.html)

  Travel from Hakone to [Kyoto](e2158.html) and stay for four nights. Consider our suggested [Kyoto itineraries](e3950.html).
* 

  [Day 8 - Side trip to Nara](e2165.html)

  On a side trip from Kyoto, visit [Nara](e2165.html), home of Japan's first permanent capital. Visit some of the oldest and largest [temples](e2058.html) in Japan and feed the deer that roam freely in the city.
* 

  [Day 9 - Kyoto to Miyajima via Himeji](e3500.html)

  Take the [shinkansen](e2018.html) from Kyoto to [Miyajima](e3401.html) and visit [Himeji Castle](e3501.html) along the way. Overnight at one of the [ryokan](e2029.html) on Miyajima.
* 

  [Day 10 - Miyajima to Kanazawa via Hiroshima](e2160.html)

  Take the ferry back to [Hiroshima](e2160.html) and see the city's sights. Continue on to [Kanazawa](e2167.html) and spend two nights there.
* 

  [Day 11 - Kanazawa](e2167.html)

  Spend a full day in Kanazawa and visit [Kenrokuen](e4200.html), one of Japan's best [landscape gardens](e2099.html) and the city's other attractions.
* 

  [Day 12 - Kanazawa to Shirakawago](e5950.html)

  Travel to [Shirakawago](e5950.html), see the nice village and stay [overnight at a gassho-zukuri farmhouse](e5952.html).
* 

  [Day 13 - Shirakawago to Takayama](e5903.html)

  Head to the beautifully preserved [old town](e5900.html) of [Takayama](e5900.html) in [Gifu Prefecture](../list/e1220.html).
* 

  [Day 14 - Takayama to Tokyo](e5900.html)

  See more of [Takayama](e5900.html) before returning to [Tokyo](e2164.html).

The above itinerary is somewhat fast-paced. Tourists preferring a slow pace of travel should consider spending more time at some of the destinations along the way.
