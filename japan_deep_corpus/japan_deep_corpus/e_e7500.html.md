Toyama City (富山) is the largest city and capital of [Toyama Prefecture](/list/e1219.html) in the Hokuriku Region of northern [Chubu](/list/e1104.html). The city is a former [castle town](e2296.html) and is historically a center of medicine. Due to its position on the [Hokuriku Shinkansen](e2018_nagano.html), Toyama is a popular stop for tourists on the way to other attractions in the area such as the [Tateyama Kurobe Alpine Route](e7550.html).
