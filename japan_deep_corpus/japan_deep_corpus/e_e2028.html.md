Unlike in much of the rest of the world, consumer prices in Japan have barely increased over the past decades. Add to it a weak [yen](e2196.html), and you have a travel destination that is much more economical than the outdated misconceptions about expensive Japan would suggest. With careful planning and a little know-how, it is now easier than ever to enjoy a budget trip in Japan.

The following budget travel guide contains the basics on how to save money on a trip to Japan. In addition, check out our [sightseeing guide](e623a.html) for information on access and admission to specific destinations and sights and get answers to your questions in our [question forum](/forum/quedisplay.html).
