The following is a full-day model course for average-paced travelers that covers the major attractions in [Kanazawa](e2167.html) on foot over about seven kilometers.

Start

Start at **Kanazawa Station**

**Walk**

15 minutes

Omicho Market

Public market famous for fresh seafood
60 minutes

[Omicho Market](e4207.html) is the largest fresh food [market](e2452.html) in [Kanazawa](e2167.html), specializing in local seafood and seasonal produce. Visitors can enjoy fresh seafood direct from the stores as well as at the numerous restaurants in the market.

**Walk**

10 minutes

Oyama Shrine

Shrine dedicated to the former feudal lord
20 minutes

[Oyama Shrine](e4203.html) is known for its unusual gate which has a mix of European and Asian influences. There is a garden with ponds and bridges on the [shrine](e2059.html) grounds that make for a pleasant stroll.

**Walk**

5 minutes

Gyokuseninmaru Garden

Reconstructed garden at Kanazawa Castle
20 minutes

Part of [Kanazawa Castle](e4202.html), the Gyokusenin Garden was relatively recently reconstructed based off archeological findings and historical records. The original [garden](e2099.html) was abandoned after the feudal system had been abolished.

**Walk**

5 minutes

Kanazawa Castle

Seat of one of Japan's most powerful feudal lords
60 minutes

Most buildings of [Kanazawa Castle](e4202.html) were after the end of the feudal age, and the only original major structures left is the Ishikawa-mon Gate which faces [Kenrokuen Garden](e4200.html). Over recent years, however, many of the former buildings have been gradually reconstructed, including a long storehouse with turrets, and two gates.

**Walk**

5 minutes

Kenrokuen Garden

One of Japan's three best landscape gardens
60-90 minutes

Take a leisurely stroll through [Kenrokuen Garden](e4200.html), and enjoy the seasonal flora and view. One of the most famous sights at the [garden](e2099.html) is the two-legged Kotojitoro Lantern at the main pond.

Direct gate between Kenrokuen and Seisonkaku

Seisonkaku Villa

Beautiful villa next to Kenrokuen Garden
30 minutes

Standing adjacent to Kenrokuen Garden, the [Seisonkaku Villa](e4201.html) was built towards the end of the feudal period and is one of the most elegant remaining [samurai](e2127.html) villas in Japan. Visitors can appreciate the traditional elements of [Japanese architecture](e2007.html) here.

**Walk**

5-10 minutes

Museums

Visit one or more of Kanazawa's outstanding museums
60 minutes

There are three outstanding [museums](e4210.html) in the vicinity of [Kenrokuen Garden](e4200.html): the [21st Century Museum of Contemporary Art](e4209.html), the [Ishikawa Prefectural History Museum](e4210.html) and the [D.T. Suzuki Museum](e4211.html). All three offer visitors a very different experience.
