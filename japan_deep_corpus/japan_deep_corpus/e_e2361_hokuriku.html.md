The **Hokuriku Area Pass** is a [rail pass](e2357.html) for exclusive use by foreign tourists. It provides four consecutive days of unlimited travel on designated trains, trams and bus lines (including unreserved seats on [shinkansen](e2018.html) and [limited express](e2016.html#category) trains) in the [Hokuriku Region](/list/e1104.html). It can be a good pass for exploring the area around [Kanazawa](e2167.html), [Kaga Onsen](e4275.html), [Fukui](e6600.html) and surroundings.

### Validity

1. Unlimited use of JR trains along the lines shown on the map above, including non-reserved seats on [shinkansen](e2018.html) and [limited express](e2016.html#category) trains.
2. Unlimited use of the Echizen Railway and the trams in [Toyama](e7500.html) and [Takaoka](e7525.html).
3. Unlimited use of the IR Ishikawa Railway between [Kanazawa](e2167.html) and Tsubata to access the JR Nanao Line, on the Ainokaze Toyama Railway between [Toyama](e7500.html) and [Takaoka](e7525.html) to access the JR Himi and Johana lines, and on the Hapi Line Fukui between [Fukui](e6600.html) and Echizen-Hanando to access the JR Kuzuryu Line; however, it is not allowed to get on and off at any stations in between. The liner surcharge for Ainokaze Liner trains is not covered.
4. Reserved seats are not covered by the pass. If pass holders wish to use a reserved seat, nevertheless, the pass will cover only the [base fare](e2359_001.html), while the [limited express fee](e2359_001.html) will have to be paid separately. Note that Kagayaki trains along the [Hokuriku Shinkansen](e2018_nagano.html) carry only reserved seating and cannot be used free of charge by pass holders.
5. Unlimited use of local JR buses around [Kanazawa](e2167.html).
6. The pass is valid on four consecutive calendar days (midnight to midnight).
7. Only foreign visitors to Japan (with [temporary visitor](e2221.html) status) can use the JR Hokuriku Area Pass. It cannot be used by residents of Japan.
8. The pass can be used only by a single person. It cannot be transferred between multiple persons. The pass holder has to carry his/her passport at all times.

### Assessment

The JR Hokuriku Area Pass is an excellent value for exploring the Hokuriku Region, as it pays off pretty easily. Use in combination with a [JR Kansai Wide Area Pass](e2361_08.html) could be worth considering.
