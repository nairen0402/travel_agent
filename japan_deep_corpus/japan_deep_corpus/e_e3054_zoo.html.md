### Zoos

* 

  Ueno Zoo

  5 minute walk from Ueno Station in [Ueno Park](e3019.html)  
  Hours: 9:30 to 17:00 (entry until 16:00)  
  Closed: Mondays (or following day if Mon is a [holiday](e2062.html)), Dec 29 to Jan 1  
  Admission: 600 yen

  Opened in 1882 in [Ueno Park](e3019.html), Ueno Zoo is Japan's oldest [zoo](e2464.html). Its most popular residents are giant panda bears, which first moved here in 1972 when relations between Japan and China were normalized.
* 

  [Zoorasia•](e3209.html)

  15 minute bus ride from Nakayama Station, [Yokohama](e2156.html)  
  Hours: 9:30 to 16:30  
  Closed: Tuesdays (or next day if Tue is a [public holiday](e2062.html)), Dec 29 to Jan 1  
  Admission: 800 yen

  [Zoorasia](e3209.html) is a well designed, modern [zoo](e2464.html), located about an hour outside of Tokyo in [Yokohama](e2156.html). The zoo has animals from around the world, including polar bears and elephants, as well as a section of animals native to Japan.

### Aquariums

* 

  [Sumida Aquarium](e3064_aquarium.html)

  At the base of [Tokyo Skytree](e3064.html)  
  Hours: 10:00 to 20:00 (weekdays), 9:00 to 21:00 (weekends)  
  Admission: 2500 yen

  The beautifully designed [Sumida Aquarium](e3064_aquarium.html) at the base of the [Tokyo Skytree](e3064.html) is relatively small, but features some outstanding exhibits, including a unique penguin enclosure and attractively presented tanks with sea life from the waters around [Tokyo](e2164.html), including the Izu and [Ogasawara](e8200.html) Islands.
* 

  Maxell Aqua Park Shinagawa

  5 minute walk from [Shinagawa Station](e3040.html)  
  Hours: 10:00 to 20:00 (longer hours during holiday seasons)  
  Admission: 2300 yen (rides extra)

  The Maxell Aqua Park Shinagawa is an [aquarium](e2425.html) just a short walk from [Shinagawa Station](e3040.html) with a variety of sealife on display.
* 

  Sunshine International Aquarium

  10 minute walk from [Ikebukuro Station](e3038.html)  
  Hours vary; typically 9:30 to 21:00 in spring and summer, and 10:00 to 18:00 in autumn and winter  
  Admission: 2600-2800 yen

  This aquarium exhibits aquatic creatures from around the world, including stingrays, sunfish, frogs, snakes, penguins, seals and otters. The aquarium is on the 10th floor of the World Import Mart Building in [Sunshine City](e3038.html#sunshine).
* 

  Tokyo Sea Life Park

  5 minute walk from [Kasai Rinkai Koen Station](e3024.html)  
  Hours: 9:30 to 17:00 (entry until 16:00)  
  Closed: Wednesdays (or next day if Wed is a [national holiday](e2062.html)), Dec 29 to Jan 1  
  Admission: 700 yen

  Located in [Kasai Rinkai Park](e3024.html), this aquarium reproduce local aquatic habitats and those from around the world. Popular attractions include its penguin and seabirds habitat as well as a 360 degree tank.
