* 

  [Day 1 - Nagoya](e2155.html)

  Spend the day in [Nagoya](e2155.html), Japan's fourth most populated city.
* 

  [Day 2 - Nagoya to Takayama](e5900.html)

  Take a [limited express](e2016.html) train to [Takayama](e5900.html) and explore its beautifully preserved [old town](e5903.html).
* 

  [Day 3 - Takayama to Kamikochi](e6040.html)

  From Takayama, take a bus to [Kamikochi](e6040.html) via [Hirayu Onsen](e5940.html). Enjoy a day in Kamikochi by hiking along one of the trails along the Azusa River. Note that Kamikochi is closed from mid November to mid April.
* 

  [Day 4 - Kamikochi to Okuhida](e5940.html)

  Head to the [Okuhida](e5940.html) region in [Gifu Prefecture](/list/e1220.html) famous for its hot springs and spend a night at a rustic onsen [ryokan](e2029.html).
* 

  [Day 5 - Okuhida to Shirakawago](e5950.html)

  Take buses from Okuhida via Takayama to [Shirakawago](e5950.html), a world heritage site because of its massive farmhouses. The best way to experience the region is to [stay overnight at one of the farmhouses](e5952.html).
* 

  [Day 6 - Shirakawago to Kanazawa](e2167.html)

  Catch a bus to [Kanazawa](e2167.html) and stay overnight in the beautiful city.
* 

  [Day 7 - Kanazawa](e2167.html)

  Spend a full day in [Kanazawa](e2167.html). Don't miss [Kenrokuen](e4200.html), one of Japan's top three landscape gardens.
* 

  [Day 8 - Alpine Route](e7550.html)

  Early in the morning, travel from [Kanazawa](e2167.html) to [Toyama](e7500.html) and traverse the [Tateyama Kurobe Alpen Route](e7550.html), a spectacular route through the [Northern Alps](e6043.html). Continue to [Nagano](e6000.html) and overnight there. Note that the Alpine Route is closed from December to mid April.
* 

  [Day 9 - Day trip to Yamanouchi](e6025.html)

  Make a day trip to [Yamanouchi](e6025.html) to see the snow monkeys in [Jigokudani Monkey Park](e6028.html).
* 

  [Day 10 - Nagano to Kiso Valley via Matsumoto](e6050.html)

  Drop by [Matsumoto](e6050.html) on your way to the [Kiso Valley](e6075.html) and see [Matsumotojo](e6051.html), one of Japan's most beautiful original castles. Continue on to the [Kiso Valley](e6075.html) and spend the night there.
* 

  [Day 11 - Kiso Valley to Nagoya](e6075.html)

  Explore [Tsumago](e6077.html) and [Magome](e6076.html) in the [Kiso Valley](e6075.html) before heading back to [Nagoya](e2155.html).

The above itinerary is somewhat fast-paced. Tourists preferring a slow pace of travel should consider spending more time at some of the destinations along the way.
