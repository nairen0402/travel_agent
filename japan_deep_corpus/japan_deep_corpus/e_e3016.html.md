Tokyo Disney Resort is comprised of the two theme parks [Tokyo Disneyland](e3016_land.html) and [Tokyo DisneySea](e3016_sea.html), the [Ikspiari](e3016_ikspiari.html) shopping mall and multiple [resort hotels](e3016_hotels.html). It is located just a 15 minute train ride from [Tokyo Station](e3037.html).

The resort is immensely popular among domestic and international visitors, and its two theme parks, Tokyo Disneyland and Tokyo DisneySea, are among the world's most visited.
