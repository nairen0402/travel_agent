JAL, ANA and Air Do operate multiple [flights](e2365.html) between [Tokyo](e2164.html)'s [Haneda Airport](e2430.html) and [Hakodate](e5350.html). The flight duration is about 80 minutes. The regular one way fare by JAL and ANA is about 43,000 yen, with [discount tickets](e2364.html) usually available from 14,000 to 25,000 yen. The fares by Air Do are typically 5-20 percent lower.

Hakodate Airport is a 20 minute [bus](e2015.html) ride (500 yen) outside of central Hakodate.

Take the JR [Tohoku](e2018_tohoku.html)/[Hokkaido](e2018_hokkaido.html) Shinkansen from [Tokyo](e2164.html) to Shin-Hakodate-Hokuto (4 hours) and transfer to the relay train to Hakodate Station in central [Hakodate](e5350.html) (20 minutes). The entire one way trip costs around 24,000 yen and takes under 5 hours. It is fully covered by the [Japan Rail Pass](e2361.html) and [JR East South Hokkaido Rail Pass](e2361_southhokkaido.html). Note that seat reservations are mandatory on [Hayabusa](e2018_tohoku.html) trains between Tokyo and Shin-Hakodate-Hokuto.

Above fees and schedules are subject to change. Be sure to check current yen [exchange rates](e2196.html).

Central [Hakodate](e5350.html) with **Hakodate Station**, the old [Motomachi district](e5351.html) and [Mount Hakodate](e5354.html) are located on the distinctively shaped peninsula. The city is served by two tram lines and a [bus](e2015.html) network. A ropeway leads to the top of [Mount Hakodate](e5354.html), which is also accessible by bus.

Hakodate's [shinkansen](e2018.html) station, **Shin-Hakodate-Hokuto Station**, is located 15 kilometers north of the city center in Hokuto City. Relay trains connect the shinkansen station with Hakodate Station in 20 minutes.

The two **tram lines**, numbered 2 and 5, share their route between Yunokawa and Jujigai and go separate ways from there towards Yachigashira (line 2) and Hakodate Dock (line 5). The fare is between 210 and 260 yen, depending on the distance traveled.

**Hakodate Airport** is located five kilometers east of the city center. Airport shuttle buses operate every 20 minutes between Hakodate Station and the airport, taking 20 minutes and costing 500 yen one way, while less frequent regular buses by Hakodate Bus do the journey in 30 minutes and for 340 yen.

The local ICAS nimoca **IC card**, as well as [Suica, Icoca and eight other IC card types](e2359_003.html) can be used on the trams and buses in Hakodate (except on the airport shuttle buses) and on JR trains between Hakodate and Shin-Hakodate-Hokuto Station.

[Onuma Quasi National Park](e5356.html) is located about 20 kilometers north of central Hakodate.

Various passes for trams and buses are available to tourists. They are available for [purchase via a smartphone](https://sp.cstm.dohna.jp/) to be shown to the driver when alighting. Only the 1-day tram pass is also available as a conventional paper ticket.

* **1-day Tram Pass** (600 yen)
    
  Unlimited use of trams in Hakodate on one calendar day (midnight to midnight). Unlike the other tickets in this list, the pass can also be purchased as a conventional paper ticket at selected sales locations, including the bus information and tourist information desks at Hakodate Station, and inside trams.
* **24-hour Tram Pass** (900 yen)
    
  Only available [digitally](https://sp.cstm.dohna.jp/), this pass provides unlimited use of trams in Hakodate for 24 hours from activation.
* **Tram and Bus Pass** (1-day: 1400 yen, 2-day: 2400 yen)
    
  Only available [digitally](https://sp.cstm.dohna.jp/), this pass provides unlimited use of trams and Hakodate Buses (not including the airport shuttle bus) on one or two calendar days (midnight to midnight).
