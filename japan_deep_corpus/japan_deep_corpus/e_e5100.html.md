Located half an hour outside of [Sendai](e5150.html), Matsushima (松島) is famous for its [bay](e5101.html), which is dotted by pine-clad islets and has been ranked one of [Japan's three most scenic views](e2250.html). The small town is also known for [Zuiganji](e5102.html), one of the [Tohoku Region](/list/e1102.html)'s most important [Zen](e2055.html) [temples](e2058.html).
