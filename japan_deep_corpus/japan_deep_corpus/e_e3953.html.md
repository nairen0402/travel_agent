Below is a list of some of [Kyoto](e2158.html)'s most popular [koyo](e2014.html) spots with the approximate timings of the best viewing in the average year. Generally speaking, they are usually best in the second half of November. See also our [nationwide list of koyo spots](e2014_where.html).

### Early season

* 

  [Takao••](e3940.html)

  Mid November

  The spectacular autumn colors around the [temples](e2058.html) in this forested valley north of Kyoto are usually at their best a few days before the autumn colors in the city center.
* 

  [Sanzenin•](e3955.html)

  Mid November

  The autumn colors in the beautiful gardens of [Sanzenin](e3955.html), a popular temple in rural [Ohara](e3932.html), a 30 minute bus ride north of [Kyoto](e2158.html) are usually at their best a few days before the trees in central Kyoto.

### Main season

* 

  [Tofukuji•••](e3930.html)

  Mid to late November

  Usually best in the second half of November, [Tofukuji Temple](e3930.html) displays some of [Kyoto](e2158.html)'s most spectacular autumn foliage. The views of and from the Tsutenkyo Bridge are particularly impressive, and the temple gets very crowded.
* 

  [Arashiyama•••](e3912.html)

  Late November to early December

  [Arashiyama](e3912.html) is a district at the outskirts of [Kyoto](e2158.html). The forested mountains in the background and the trees on the grounds of Arashiyama's temples usually turn colors in the second half of November. [Tenryuji](e3913.html), Jojakkoji, Nisonin and Gioji are some of the best temples for autumn colors.
* 

  [Kiyomizudera••](e3901.html)

  Late November to early December

  Illumination 2025: 11/22 to 12/7, 17:30 to 21:30 (entry until 21:00), 500 yen

  The highly popular [Kiyomizudera](e3901.html) is also a spectacular autumn color spot. The trees around the temples large wooded stages usually turn red in the second half of November.
* 

  [Eikando••](e3956.html)

  Late November to early December

  Illumination 2025: 11/15 to 12/10, 17:30 to 21:00 (entry until 20:30), 700 yen

  With their abundance of autumn colors, the temple grounds of [Eikando](e3956.html) are another of [Kyoto](e2158.html)'s most popular koyo spots. It is one of several temples that offer special illumination opening hours in the evenings.
* 

  [Yoshiminedera••](e3947.html)

  Mid to late November

  Similar to [Kiyomizudera](e3901.html) on Kyoto's eastern mountains (Higashiyama), [Yoshiminedera](e3947.html) is a temple on the slopes of Kyoto's western mountains (Nishiyama) with nice views over the city and beautiful autumn colors.
* 

  [Shugakuin Imperial Villa••](e3936.html)

  Mid to late November

  The spacious and elegant gardens of the [Shugakuin Imperial Villa](e3936.html) feature beautiful autumn colors during the second half of November. It can be visited only on tours, and advance reservations are mandatory.
* 

  [Kodaiji•](e3927.html)

  Late November to early December

  Illumination 2025: 10/24 to 12/14, 17:00 to 22:00 (entry until 21:30), 600 yen

  [Kodaiji Temple](e3927.html), not far from [Kiyomizudera](e3901.html), is also known for its evening illuminations. The sight of the lit up maple leaves, reflecting on the surface of the temple's pond, are particuarly breathtaking.
* 

  [Shinnyodo•](e3970.html)

  Mid to late November

  This lesser known temple in a calm residential neighborhood not too far from [Ginkakuji](e3907.html) and the [Philosopher Trail](e3906.html) is a favorite autumn color spot among the locals. [Shinnyodo](e3970.html)'s temple grounds feature a 3-story pagoda and dozens of maple trees that turn spectacular in late November.
* 

  [Enkoji•](e3969.html)

  Mid to late November

  2025: [online reservations](https://www.enkouji.jp/en/) are necessary to visit Enkoji between 11/10 and 12/10

  A small [temple](e2058.html) within walking distance of [Shugakuin Imperial Villa](e3936.html) (see above), [Enkoji](e3969.html) offers beautiful autumn colors. Be sure not to miss the spectacular bird's view of the temple from the hill behind.
* 

  [Daigoji•](e3916.html)

  Mid to late November

  The highlight at [Daigoji](e3916.html) is the Bentendo Hall in the back of the Shimo Daigo temple compex, surrounded by a pond and lots of maple trees.
* 

  [Nanzenji•](e3905.html)

  Late November to early December

  Besides [Nanzenji Temple](e3905.html)'s main grounds, the subtemples Nazenin and Tenjuan are particularly popular autumn color spots.
* 

  [Kitano Tenmangu](e3939.html)

  Mid November to early December

  Illumination 2025: 11/1 to 12/7, sunset to 20:00 (entry until 19:40), 1200 yen

  [Kitano Tenmangu](e3939.html) opens its maple tree garden to the public every fall. Illuminations are held during the peak of the autumn leaf season. The admission fee includes a cup of green tea and a snack.
* 

  [Chionin](e3928.html)

  Late November to early December

  Illumination 2025: 11/19 to 12/7, 17:30 to 21:30 (entry until 21:00), 800 yen

  The massive head temple of the [Buddhist](e2055.html) Jodo sect attracts autumn leaf viewers by illuminating its freely accessible temple grounds and its Yuzen garden, for which an admission fee applies.
* 

  [Shorenin](e3954.html)

  Late November to early December

  Illumination 2025: 11/7 to 12/7, 18:00 to 22:00 (entry until 21:30), 1000 yen

  After a 5-year hiatus due to renovation works, Shorenin Temple in the [Higashiyama District](e3959.html) resumes its evening autumn illuminations this year.
