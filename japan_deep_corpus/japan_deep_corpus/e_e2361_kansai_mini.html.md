The **JR Kansai Mini Pass** is a [rail pass](e2357.html) for exclusive use by foreign tourists, which provides three consecutive days of unlimited travel on local and rapid JR trains in the [Kansai Region](/list/e1105.html) around [Osaka](e2157.html), [Kyoto](e2158.html), [Kobe](e2159.html) and [Nara](e2165.html).

### Validity

1. Unlimited use of [local](e2016.html#category) and [rapid](e2016.html#category) JR trains in the coverage area shown on the map above. The pass is not valid on [limited express](e2016.html#category) trains and [shinkansen](e2018.html).
2. The pass is valid on three consecutive calendar days (midnight to midnight).
3. Only foreign visitors to Japan (with [temporary visitor](e2221.html) status) can use the pass. It cannot be used by residents of Japan.
4. The pass can be used only by a single person. It cannot be transferred between multiple persons. The pass holder has to carry his/her passport at all times.

### Points of sale

The JR Kansai Mini Pass is sold only [online](https://www.klook.com/activity/18356-jr-kansai-mini-pass/?aid=12675) through various travel websites. The actual pass has to be picked up at a major station in the area covered before use, including [Kansai Airport](e2033.html), [Osaka](e4020.html), Shin-Osaka, [Kyoto](e3922.html), [Sannomiya](e2159.html) or [Nara](e2165.html).

### Assessment

This pass can pay off for travelers who make multiple day trips out of a base across the Greater Osaka/Kyoto/Kobe area or otherwise use JR trains a lot over a 3-day period. For comparison, below is a list of regular JR trains fares for some popular trips:

* Kyoto-Nara round trip: 1440 yen
* Kyoto-Osaka round trip: 1160 yen
* Kyoto-Kobe round trip: 2220 yen
* Osaka-Nara round trip: 1640 yen
* Osaka-Kobe round trip: 920 yen

### Alternative passes

The [JR Kansai Area Pass](e2361_03.html) has a wider coverage area than the Kansai Mini Pass and costs 2800 yen for a 1-day pass or 5800 yen for a 3-day pass.
