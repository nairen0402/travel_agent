Akita City (秋田) is the capital of [Akita Prefecture](/list/e1203.html), located up north in the [Tohoku Region](/list/e1102.html) of Japan. The city is best known for hosting the [Kanto Matsuri](e3627.html), a large [festival](e2063.html) in early August, in which revelers balance long bamboo poles with dozens of paper lanterns attached to the end.
