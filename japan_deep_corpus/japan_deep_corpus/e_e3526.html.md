Take the [JR Tokaido Shinkansen](e2018_tokaido.html) from [Tokyo](e2164.html) to [Kyoto](e2158.html) and transfer to a JR [limited express](e2016.html) train to Kinosaki Onsen Station. The one way journey takes 5-6 hours and costs around 18,000 yen. The [Japan Rail Pass](e2361.html) fully covers the trip if a Hikari train is used between Tokyo and Kyoto, but a supplement fee is required is using a Nozomi train.

[How to get from Tokyo to Osaka](e4006.html)

Konotori Tajima Airport is the closest airport to [Kinosaki](e3525.html) (45 minute, 500 yen by bus). However, the airport is connected only to Osaka's [Itami Airport](e2433.html) with two flights per day by JAL. Flight duration is 40 minutes. The regular one way fare is about 17,000 yen, but by using a [discount offer](e2364.html), you can usually fly for between 7,000 and 9,000 yen.

Tottori Airport is the closest airport with direct [flights](e2365.html) to Tokyo ([Haneda Airport](e2430.html)). ANA operates multiple flights per day. Flight duration is 80 minutes. The regular one way fare is about 37,000 yen, but by using a [discount offer](e2364.html), you can usually fly for between 16,000 and 33,000 yen.

From Tottori Airport, [Kinosaki](e3525.html) can be reached in about two hours by [rental car](e2024.html). Alternatively, take the airport bus from the airport to Tottori Station (20 minutes, 480 yen) and use local trains along the JR Sanin Line to reach Kinosaki (2.5 hours, 1340 yen, one transfer along the way).

[Kinosaki](e3525.html) is a compact town and can be fully explored on foot. It takes about 15-25 minutes to walk from the train station at the eastern end of town to the [Kono-yu bath house](e3527.html) at the western end of town.

Konotori Tajima Airport is the closest [airport](e2365.html) to Kinosaki, a 45 minute bus ride south of Kinosaki (500 yen one way) near the city center of Toyooka City. The [Konotori no Sato](e3530.html) Oriental Stork Sanctuary is also located close to Toyooka city center.
