Located in the center of [Hokkaido](/list/e1101.html), Asahikawa (旭川) is the island's second largest city after [Sapporo](e2163.html). The city is not known as a leading tourist destination, but its zoo, [Asahiyama Zoo](e6892.html), is among Japan's best. The local [noodle](e2042.html) dish, [Asahikawa Ramen](e6893.html), is also quite well known.
