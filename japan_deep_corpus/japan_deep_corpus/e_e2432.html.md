Kobe Airport (神戸空港, Kōbe Kūkō, airport code: UKB) is located on a man-made island just off the coast of central [Kobe](e2159.html). It was opened in 2006.

Kobe Airport offers [domestic flights](e2365.html) by multiple airlines at terminal 1, and a small number of [international flights](e2034.html) to nearby Asian destinations at terminal 2.

### Access to Kobe Airport

* 

  Port Liner

  The Port Liner, an elevated train, connects Kobe Airport with Kobe's main railway station, Sannomiya Station, in less than 20 minutes. The one way fare is 340 yen.
* 

  Taxi

  A [taxi](e2021.html) ride into central Kobe costs arround 4000 yen.
